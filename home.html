About Pixel Paint

Pixel Paint was created with a simple mission: to make pixel art accessible to everyone. Whether you're a professional game developer creating sprites for your next indie game, a digital artist exploring the retro aesthetic, or a complete beginner wanting to try something new, Pixel Paint provides the tools you need.

Our editor combines simplicity with power. The clean, intuitive interface means you can start creating within seconds, while advanced features like multi-layer support and the bucket fill tool give experienced artists the control they need. Everything runs directly in your browser with no installation required.

Pixel art has seen a massive resurgence in recent years, from indie games to digital collectibles. The constraints of the medium encourage creativity and often result in artwork that's both nostalgic and timeless. With Pixel Paint, you have everything you need to join this vibrant creative community.

We believe that powerful creative tools should be free and accessible. That's why Pixel Paint is completely free to use with no hidden costs, no watermarks, and no limits on what you can create. Your art belongs to you, and we're just here to provide the canvas.
